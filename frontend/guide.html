Use Guide

Introduction

This site has been created to assist you with calculating your Flee The Facility (FTF) inventory as

Taking Screenshots

  • Take clear screenshots of your inventory pages
  • Make sure item names are clearly visible
  • Ensure quantity numbers are readable
  • You can upload multiple screenshots at once

Processing Results

  • After uploading, click "Calculate" to process your images
  • Review the extracted items and quantities
  • Use the edit feature to make any necessary corrections
  • Values are shown in both FV (Face Value) and HV (High Value)

Tips

  • For best results, use full-screen screenshots
  • Avoid overlapping windows or UI elements
  • You can edit quantities and remove items if needed
  • Save your results for future reference
